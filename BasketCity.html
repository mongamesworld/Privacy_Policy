Privacy Policy - Basket City

Effective: 01/06/2025

1. Data Collection

We don't directly collect personal data, but third-party ad providers (like Google AdMob) may collect data as described below.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA)
  • Non-personal usage data (ad interactions)
  • Device type and OS version

This data is used under Google's policies: Google Privacy Policy.

3. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

  • Legal Basis: Your consent
  • Your Rights: Access, rectify, or delete data via Google's settings

4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Opt-out of data "sale": Google AdMob (our ad partner) may use your data for ads. This is considered a "sale" under CCPA. Click here to opt-out or use the in-game button in Settings.
  • Request deletion: Email us at mongamesworld@gmail.com with "CCPA Deletion Request". We respond within 45 days.
  • Non-discrimination: You will not be penalized for exercising CCPA rights.
  • Verified requests: We may need to verify your identity before processing (e.g., confirm email/device ID).
