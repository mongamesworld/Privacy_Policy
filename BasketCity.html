Privacy Policy - Basket City

Last Updated: 17/06/2025

At a Glance: Basket City respects your privacy. We don't directly collect personal data but use Google AdMob for ads. We comply with GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) through Google's consent tools.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Data We Don't Collect

We do not directly collect or store:

  • Names, emails, or contact info
  • Payment information
  • Location data (GPS)

1.2 Data Processed by Services

Third parties (like Google AdMob) may process anonymous data:

  • Device Info: Model, OS version
  • Usage Data: Session length, features used
  • Advertising ID: Google's AAID

1.3 Consent Management

Google UMP SDK: We use Google's User Messaging Platform to:
  • Display GDPR/CCPA-compliant consent requests
  • Respect "Do Not Sell" preferences (CCPA)
  • Store proof of obtained consent
  • Block data processing until consent is given

2. Legal Compliance

2.1 GDPR (EU/EEA)

For European users:

  • Legal Basis: Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) for personalized ads
  • Withdrawal: Change preferences in app settings
  • DPO Contact: mongamesworld@gmail.com

2.2 CCPA (California)

California residents can:

  • Opt-out of data "sales" via Privacy Rights webpage
  • Request disclosure of data collected in past 12 months

3. Your Rights

  • CA Users: Click here to opt-out of ad personalization
